ICON PLACEHOLDER

To use the custom Frozen Peak Solutions icon, you need to generate a PNG file:

Quick Steps:
1. Open build/icon.svg in a browser
2. Right-click and "Save As" or take a screenshot
3. Save as build/icon.png (512x512 recommended)

OR use an online converter:
1. Go to https://cloudconvert.com/svg-to-png
2. Upload build/icon.svg
3. Set size to 512x512
4. Download as build/icon.png

OR use ImageMagick:
convert build/icon.svg -resize 512x512 build/icon.png

Once icon.png exists, the app will use it for the window icon.
